What is AI-Powered Lead Generation?

AI-powered lead generation is the use of artificial intelligence to help businesses find, organize, and understand potential customers. Instead of manually reviewing every message or contact, AI tools can help identify which leads may be most ready for follow-up.

This topic connects to computer science because it uses web applications, databases, automation, algorithms, and natural language processing.

Why It Matters

Businesses often receive many messages from people with different levels of interest. AI can help sort these conversations so teams can respond faster and more effectively.
