What is Lead Qualification?

Lead qualification is the process of deciding how interested or ready a potential customer may be. In a business setting, not every lead has the same level of interest. Some people are ready to talk right away, while others may only be researching.

AI can help organize leads by looking at patterns in messages, timing, and customer responses. This can help a team decide who may need a faster follow-up.

Types of Leads

  • Hot Lead: Someone who may be ready soon.
  • Warm Lead: Someone interested but still deciding.
  • Cold Lead: Someone with little or unclear interest.
  • Do Not Contact: Someone who should not receive more messages.

Why AI Helps

AI does not replace people, but it can support them by helping sort large amounts of information. This is useful when a business has many conversations happening at the same time.
